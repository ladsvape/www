Propylene glycol / Vegetable glycerine

  • Vegetable glycerine (VG) is what gives cloud in vaping, the more VG the thicker the cloud.
  • Propylene glycol (PG) creates the throat hit which gives the sensation from smoking. The more PG the stronger the hit.
PG should not be vaped without at least a concentration of 40% VG, other which the hit would be far too strong. PG is very thin in comparison to VG thus we recommend a ratio of 30% PG to 70% VG.
